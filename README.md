# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Food Log**, developed by **Toad Frog**, handles information when you use our Android application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We do **not** collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) directly. The App does not require account registration, login, or submission of any personal details.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

### Firebase Analytics (Google LLC)

The App uses **Firebase Analytics**, a service provided by Google LLC, to help us understand how users interact with the App. Firebase Analytics may automatically collect certain non-personally identifiable information, including:

- Device type, model, and operating system version
- App version and session duration
- General usage patterns and feature interactions
- Crash reports and performance data
- Approximate country-level location (derived from IP address, not GPS)

This data is collected anonymously and aggregated. We do not use it to identify individual users.

For more information on how Google handles this data, please review:
- [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

---

## 3. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data on our own servers. Analytics data collected by Firebase is subject to Google's own retention policies, which you can review at the links above.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to address it promptly.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
